Rules

1. Being rude or arguing with other players in a hostile manner is not allowed.

2. Malicious threats of any kind towards other players are not permitted.

3. Do not kill pets, animals, or any name-tagged mob of another player.

4. Going into another player’s base without permission is trespassing.

5. Profanity, racism, and discrimination are prohibited in public chat.

6. Griefing is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.

7. Impersonating staff members or other players is not allowed.

8. Crafting or using TNT in the server is not allowed.

9. Keep the chat civil and appropriate for everyone.

10. Do not loot another player’s death location.

11. Stealing other player’s items is not allowed.

12. Please keep the chat friendly for all ages.


13. Hacking is not allowed.
